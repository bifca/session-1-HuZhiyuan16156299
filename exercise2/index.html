Pokemon Evolutionary Chain

Charmander

The lesser fire dragon is a biped-like pokemon that resembles a theropod.The baby dragon was orange in color, with a creamy underbelly and tail.Its eyes are blue and its four small teeth are separated in its upper and lower jaw.It has short limbs, four very short fingers and three PAWS.

Charmeleon

Fire dinosaurs were similar in shape to small fire dragons, with darker skin tones and more vivid eyes.It has a horn-shaped bulge on the top of its head and a pointed beak that resembles a bird's beak.Its limbs and tail were longer than those of the little dragon, its claws were longer and sharper, and the end of its tail was still burning with fire.

Charizard

Fire-breathing dragon is a bipedal walking pokemon with western dragon characteristics.The skin is mainly orange, lighter than that of fire dinosaurs, and creamy from the abdomen to the tip of the tail and the soles of the feet.It has short, thick lower limbs and relatively small upper limbs with three sharp claws and three sharp claws on its feet.

Charizard-Mega X

The super fire-breathing dragon X's limbs are similar to the original, but the main skin color turns gray-black and the mandible to the tip of the tail turns sky blue.There were blue claw-like projections on each side of the shoulders, and small fin-like projections on the back of the neck.The inside of the wing turns dark blue, the lower end of the wing divides into a few curved corners, and the protruding shape of the wing turns into a sharp claw.

Charizard-Mega Y

The super fire-breathing dragon Y has a similar color scheme to the original, except the creamy part starts with the chin.It has a third horn in the middle at the back of its head, which is longer than both.Its neck and mouth became shorter, while its limbs, tail and wings became longer.

Charizard-Gigantamax

The power of fire inside the hypertrophine was strengthened, so that not only its tail but also its mouth and horns erupted in flames.These flames burn more vigorously when the dragon roars.
